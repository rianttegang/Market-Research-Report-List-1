<p><p>The high barrier food containers market can be divided into two main types: degradable and non-degradable. Degradable containers are designed to break down and decompose over time, often made from materials like bioplastics, which promote environmental sustainability. Non-degradable containers, on the other hand, are typically made from traditional plastics or metals that provide excellent barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and light, ensuring longer shelf life but raising concerns about environmental impact due to their persistence in landfills.</p></p>
