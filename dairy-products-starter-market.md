<p><p>The dairy products starter market is categorized into three main types: mesophilic, thermophilic, and probiotic starters. Mesophilic starters thrive at moderate temperatures and are commonly used for producing cheese and yogurt. Thermophilic starters operate at higher temperatures, essential for making yogurt and specific cheeses, enhancing texture and flavor. Probiotic starters contain live microorganisms that confer health benefits, aiding digestion and boosting immunity, thus catering to the growing demand for functional dairy products in health-conscious markets.</p></p>
