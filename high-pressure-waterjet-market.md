<p><p>The high pressure waterjet market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Impingement Jet**: Focuses water pressure on a small area, ideal for cutting and surface preparation due to its concentrated force. </p><p>2. **Continuous Jet**: Provides a steady stream of water for applications like cleaning and surface treatment, ensuring uniform coverage and efficiency. </p><p>3. **Mixing Jet**: Combines high-pressure water with additives for enhanced cleaning and cutting capabilities, often used in industrial settings for improved effectiveness and versatility.</p></p>
