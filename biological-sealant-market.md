<p><p>The biological sealant market encompasses various types, including fibrin-based and collagen-based sealants, among others. Fibrin sealants mimic the final stages of blood coagulation, promoting quick wound closure and tissue healing. Collagen-based sealants leverage collagen's natural properties to enhance adhesion and regeneration in soft tissues. Other types may include synthetic or plant-based options tailored for specific medical applications. Collectively, these sealants are used in surgical procedures, trauma care, and wound management, enhancing patient outcomes through effective tissue bonding.</p></p>
