<p><p>Label-free array systems encompass various technologies for real-time biomolecular interactions without requiring labels. Surface Plasmon Resonance (SPR) detects changes in refractive index near a sensor surface, indicating binding events. Bio-layer Interferometry (BLI) monitors interference patterns to measure binding kinetics and affinity. Cellular Dielectric Spectroscopy analyzes dielectric properties of cells, providing insights into cellular interactions and health. Other emerging methods include acoustic waves and opto-fluidic systems, each offering distinct advantages for studying complex biological processes in research and development settings.</p></p>
