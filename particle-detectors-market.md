<p><p>The particle detectors market encompasses various types, including ionization detectors, scintillation detectors, and others. Ionization detectors operate by measuring charged particles produced when radiation ionizes a gas. Scintillation detectors use materials that emit light when excited by radiation, allowing for efficient detection of high-energy particles. Other detectors include semiconductor detectors, Cherenkov detectors, and time-of-flight detectors, each with unique mechanisms suited for specific applications, such as medical imaging, nuclear industry monitoring, and scientific research in particle physics.</p></p>
