<p><p>Isoparaffin solvents are categorized based on their carbon chain lengths, including C8, C12, C16, C20, and others. C8 is used for lighter applications like paints and coatings, offering low evaporation rates. C12 serves in industrial cleaning and degreasing, providing effective solvency. C16 is suitable for lubricants and metalworking fluids, contributing to film strength. C20 finds use in higher viscosity applications and specialty formulations. Other categories encompass various blends serving niche markets and specific solvent requirements within diverse industries.</p></p>
