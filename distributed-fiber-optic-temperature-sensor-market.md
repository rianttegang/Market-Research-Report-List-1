<p><p>The Distributed Fiber Optic Temperature Sensor market mainly comprises two types: Multi-Mode Fiber and Single-Mode Fiber. Multi-Mode Fiber sensors utilize fibers that can carry multiple light modes, enabling effective temperature monitoring over shorter distances, ideal for commercial applications. In contrast, Single-Mode Fiber sensors use a narrower core, allowing a single light path, which facilitates longer sensing distances and higher precision, making them suitable for industrial and critical infrastructure monitoring. Both types enhance temperature measurement capabilities across various sectors.</p></p>
