<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cereal production equipment is categorized primarily into semi-automatic and automatic systems. Semi-automatic equipment requires manual intervention for certain tasks, providing flexibility and lower initial costs, making it suitable for small to medium-scale operations. In contrast, automatic equipment streamlines the entire process with minimal human intervention, improving efficiency, consistency, and productivity, ideal for large-scale operations. The choice between these types depends on production volume, budget, and operational capabilities, driving the dynamics of the cereal production equipment market.</p></p>
