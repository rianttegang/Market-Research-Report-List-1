<p><strong>The Instant Pot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-pressure</li><li>High-pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Instant Pot market can be categorized into low-pressure and high-pressure segments based on cooking capabilities. Low-pressure models typically operate around 4-6 psi, suitable for gentle cooking methods like steaming or simmering, ideal for delicate foods. In contrast, high-pressure models function at 10-15 psi, enabling faster cooking times and efficient results for a variety of dishes, including tougher meats and grains. Consumers choose between these types based on their cooking preferences, speed requirements, and types of meals they prepare.</p></p>
