<p><p>The Inflammatory Bowel Disease (IBD) therapeutics market features several key categories. Anti-inflammatory biologics, such as monoclonal antibodies, target specific pathways in the immune system to reduce inflammation effectively. Non-Steroidal Anti-inflammatory Drugs (NSAIDs) provide symptomatic relief by alleviating pain and inflammation but must be used cautiously to avoid complications. Corticosteroids are powerful anti-inflammatory agents that quickly control severe flare-ups but carry risks of long-term side effects. Together, these treatments aim to manage IBD symptoms and improve patients' quality of life.</p></p>
