<p><strong>The Automotive Die Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Work Die Steel</li><li>Hot Work Die Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive die steel market is primarily divided into two types: cold work die steel and hot work die steel. Cold work die steel is used for tools that operate at room temperature, ideal for processes like stamping and shearing, providing high wear resistance and toughness. In contrast, hot work die steel is designed for high-temperature applications, such as forging and extrusion, where it maintains strength and durability under extreme heat. Both types are essential for efficient manufacturing in the automotive industry.</p></p>
