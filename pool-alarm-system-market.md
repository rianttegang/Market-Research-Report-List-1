<p><strong>The Pool Alarm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fence/Wall Mounted Pool Alarm</li><li>Cell-Mounted Infrared Detector</li><li>Underwater Motion Alert</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pool alarm system market encompasses various types designed to enhance safety around swimming pools. Fence or wall-mounted pool alarms are installed to detect unauthorized entry, triggering alerts when someone approaches the pool area. Cell-mounted infrared detectors use infrared technology to monitor movements near the water's edge, providing immediate notifications. Underwater motion alerts are submerged sensors that detect movement within the pool, ensuring that even activities beneath the surface are monitored for safety, helping to prevent accidents or drownings.</p></p>
