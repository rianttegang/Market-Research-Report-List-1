<p><p>The Nickel Oxide Sputtering Target market is primarily divided into two types: Rotatable Transformation and Non-Rotating Type. Conversely, non-rotating types are simpler and often used for smaller applications or specific coating requirements. Their choice depends on the application's scale and precision needs.</p></p>
