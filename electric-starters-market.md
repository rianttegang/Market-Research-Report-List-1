<p><p>The electric starters market primarily includes two main types: manual starters and magnetic motor starters. Manual starters require operators to physically engage the motor using a switch or lever, ideal for small motors in less demanding applications. In contrast, magnetic motor starters automate the starting process using electromagnets, offering enhanced control, protection, and convenience for larger, more complex systems. These types cater to different operational needs, influencing industries from manufacturing to HVAC, by providing tailored solutions for motor control.</p></p>
