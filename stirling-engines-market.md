<p><strong>The Stirling Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alpha Stirling Engines</li><li>Beta Stirling Engines</li><li>Gamma Stirling Engines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Stirling engine market is segmented into three main types: Alpha, Beta, and Gamma Stirling engines. Alpha Stirling engines feature separate hot and cold cylinders, offering high efficiency and power output, ideal for power generation. Beta Stirling engines have a single cylinder with a displacer and piston, suitable for low-power applications like solar energy systems. Gamma Stirling engines, also featuring a separate displacer and power piston, are versatile and often used in refrigeration systems. Each type suits different applications and industries.</p></p>
