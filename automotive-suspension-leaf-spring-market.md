<p><ul><li>Mono Leaf Springs</li><li>Multi Leaf Springs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive suspension leaf spring market consists primarily of two types: mono leaf springs and multi leaf springs. Mono leaf springs consist of a single, flat strip of metal, providing a lightweight and compact solution, ideal for applications requiring reduced weight without sacrificing performance. In contrast, multi leaf springs are composed of several layered strips, offering enhanced strength and load-bearing capacity, making them suitable for heavier vehicles. Both types play a crucial role in vehicle stability, comfort, and overall ride quality.</p></p>
