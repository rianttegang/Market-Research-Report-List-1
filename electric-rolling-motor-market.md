<p><p>The electric rolling motor market comprises two main types: non-torque and torque types. Non-torque type motors focus on providing consistent speed and efficiency without emphasizing rotational force, making them suitable for applications requiring steady motion. In contrast, torque type motors are designed to deliver higher rotational force, making them ideal for applications needing strong acceleration and heavy lifting capabilities. Both types cater to different operational needs, influencing their adoption in various sectors such as automotive and industrial automation.</p></p>
