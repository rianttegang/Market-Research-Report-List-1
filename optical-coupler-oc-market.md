<p><p>Optical couplers (OCs) are essential components in fiber optics that facilitate the coupling of light between different fibers or into single fibers. The market for optical couplers is categorized into two primary types: linear and non-linear. Linear optical couplers maintain a proportional relationship between input and output signals, ensuring consistent transmission quality. Non-linear optical couplers, on the other hand, involve mechanisms where the output signal can vary non-proportionally, allowing for enhanced signal processing and modulation capabilities, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
