<p><p>The Microwave Wireless Monitoring System market is primarily segmented into analog and digital microwave wireless video transmission. Analog microwave wireless video transmission utilizes continuous signals for real-time video transfer, offering simplicity and immediate response. However, it is susceptible to interference and lower image quality. In contrast, digital microwave wireless video transmission converts video into digital packets, enhancing signal integrity and enabling advanced features like encryption and compression. This approach provides superior image quality, greater transmission range, and improved security for monitoring applications.</p></p>
