<p><p>The laboratory tube furnace market is primarily segmented into single zone and multi-zone furnaces. Single zone furnaces offer uniform heating within a single temperature zone, making them ideal for basic applications requiring consistent heat distribution. In contrast, multi-zone furnaces feature multiple heating sections that allow for varying temperatures along the tube, catering to complex processes that require temperature gradients. This segmentation enables users to select equipment based on specific experimental needs, enhancing efficiency and precision in thermal treatments.</p></p>
