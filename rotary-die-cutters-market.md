<p><p>The rotary die cutters market is primarily segmented into semi-rotary and full rotary die cutters. Semi-rotary die cutters utilize a combination of rotary and flatbed techniques, allowing for more flexibility in producing intricate designs on various substrates. In contrast, full rotary die cutters operate exclusively in a continuous rotary motion, offering higher speeds and efficiency for mass production of uniform shapes. Both types cater to diverse applications in packaging, labels, and printing, focusing on precision and speed.</p></p>
