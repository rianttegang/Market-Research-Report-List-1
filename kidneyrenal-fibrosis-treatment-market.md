<p><p>The Kidney/Renal Fibrosis Treatment Market comprises various therapeutic classes aimed at managing renal fibrosis. Angiotensin II Receptor Blockers (ARBs) help reduce blood pressure and oxidative stress, while Angiotensin Converting Enzyme (ACE) Inhibitors lower blood pressure and decrease proteinuria. Pirfenidone is an anti-fibrotic agent that targets inflammation and fibrosis. Renin Inhibitors focus on blocking the renin-angiotensin pathway, and Vasopeptidase Inhibitors combine ACE inhibition with neutral endopeptidase inhibition, aiming to improve renal outcomes and mitigate fibrosis progression.</p></p>
