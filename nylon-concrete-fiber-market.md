<p><p>The Nylon Concrete Fiber market is segmented into three main types: Monofilament Fibers, Multifilament Fibers, and Fibrillated Fibers. Monofilament Fibers consist of single strands that enhance tensile strength and crack resistance. Multifilament Fibers are made up of multiple intertwined strands, offering improved flexibility and load distribution. Fibrillated Fibers are formed from thin plastic sheets, creating a network that enhances bonding within the concrete mix. Each type serves specific applications, improving the durability and performance of concrete structures.</p></p>
