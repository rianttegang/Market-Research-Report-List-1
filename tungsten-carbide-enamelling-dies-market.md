Horizontal enamelling dies are designed for applications requiring a side-to-side orientation, facilitating efficient enamel application and heat distribution. Conversely, vertical enamelling dies are oriented upright, optimizing space and allowing for gravity-assisted processes. Both types offer durability and precision, making them essential in the enamelling industry for producing high-quality, long-lasting products. Their selection depends on specific operational requirements and production workflows.</p></p>
