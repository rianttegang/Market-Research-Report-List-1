<p><ul><li>Fan-forced</li><li>Infrared</li><li>Ceramic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric garage heater market comprises three main types: fan-forced, infrared, and ceramic heaters. Fan-forced heaters utilize a fan to circulate warm air quickly, making them suitable for larger spaces. Infrared heaters emit radiant heat, directly warming objects and people, ideal for spot heating. Ceramic heaters use ceramic plates for efficient heating and are compact, offering quick warmth in small areas. Each type caters to different heating needs, preferences, and garage sizes, providing effective temperature control during colder months.</p></p>
