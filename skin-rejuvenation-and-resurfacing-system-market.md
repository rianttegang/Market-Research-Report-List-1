<p><p>The skin rejuvenation and resurfacing system market comprises primarily two types: ablative lasers and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layer of skin, promoting collagen production and enhancing skin texture and tone. They are effective for deeper wrinkles and scars but involve longer recovery times. In contrast, non-ablative lasers target the underlying skin without damaging the surface, leading to less downtime and mild effects. Both methods cater to varying patient needs and preferences for skin improvement.</p></p>
