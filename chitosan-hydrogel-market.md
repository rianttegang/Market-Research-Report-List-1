<p><p>Chitosan hydrogels are categorized into two main types: injection type and non-injection type. Injection type hydrogels are designed for easy injection into the body, often used in medical applications like drug delivery and tissue engineering. They offer convenience and precise administration. Non-injection type hydrogels, on the other hand, are applied topically or used in bulk applications, such as wound dressings and bioengineering. Both types leverage chitosan’s biocompatibility and biodegradability, making them suitable for various healthcare applications.</p></p>
