<p><p>The Single-Phase Smart Meter market encompasses two primary types: Direct Current (DC) Meters and Alternating Current (AC) Meters. DC Meters are designed to measure electricity in systems that utilize direct current, commonly found in renewable energy applications like solar panels. In contrast, AC Meters measure alternating current, which is the standard for most electric grids and residential applications. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing energy monitoring and management, enabling improved efficiency and data analysis for utilities and consumers.</p></p>
