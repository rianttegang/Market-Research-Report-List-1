<p><p>Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) equipment enables the simultaneous transmission of multiple data channels over a single optical fiber by utilizing different wavelengths (or colors) of laser light. The market comprises two primary types: Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing (CWDM) equipment, which typically uses wider channel spacing and is cost-effective for shorter distances; and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing (DWDM) equipment, which allows for closer channel spacing, facilitating greater bandwidth and longer-distance transmissions. Both are crucial for enhancing network capacity and efficiency.</p></p>
