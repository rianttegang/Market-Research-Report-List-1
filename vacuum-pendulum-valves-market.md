<p><p>The vacuum pendulum valve market comprises three main types: manual, pneumatic, and electromagnetic pendulum valves. Manual pendulum valves operate through manual control, providing simplicity and reliability in low-tech applications. Pneumatic pendulum valves utilize compressed air for actuation, enabling faster response times and automation in industrial settings. Electromagnetic pendulum valves employ electromagnetic mechanisms for precise control and operation, offering high efficiency and integration with advanced control systems. Together, these valve types cater to diverse industrial needs in vacuum systems.</p></p>
