<p><ul><li>Emulsion PCR</li><li>Bridge Amplification</li><li>Single-molecule</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gene sequencing market comprises various technologies, including Emulsion PCR, Bridge Amplification, and Single-Molecule Sequencing. Emulsion PCR involves partitioning DNA into droplets for parallel amplification, enhancing throughput and accuracy. Bridge Amplification utilizes a solid surface to create clonal clusters of DNA, facilitating high-density sequencing. Single-Molecule Sequencing directly reads individual DNA molecules without amplification, providing rapid insights and longer read lengths. Each method offers distinct advantages, catering to diverse research needs and applications within genomics.</p></p>
