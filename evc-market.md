<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Charging (EVC) market comprises two primary types: on-board and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are integrated within the vehicle, converting AC power from the grid into DC power for battery charging, offering convenience and compactness. Off-board chargers, however, are located externally, such as at charging stations, capable of delivering higher power levels for faster charging. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing factors like charging speed, infrastructure requirements, and overall vehicle design.</p></p>
