<p><strong>The Pressure-Volume Loop Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ejection Fraction</li><li>Max and Min Ventricular dP/dt</li><li>Cardiac Output</li><li>Contractility Index</li><li>Elastance</li><li>Stroke Volume</li><li>Pre-load Recruitable Stroke Work</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure-volume loop systems are essential for assessing cardiac function and hemodynamics. Key market types include ejection fraction, which measures the percentage of blood ejected during contraction; maximum and minimum ventricular dP/dt, indicating the rate of pressure change in the heart; and cardiac output, reflecting total blood pumped. Contractility index gauges the heart's contractile strength, while elastance assesses chamber stiffness. Stroke volume represents blood volume per beat, and pre-load recruitable stroke work evaluates the efficiency of stroke volume changes with preload variations.</p></p>
