<p><strong>The FLNG Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LNG FPSO</li><li>FSRU</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The FLNG market encompasses two primary types: LNG FPSO (Floating Production Storage and Offloading) and FSRU (Floating Storage and Regasification Unit). LNG FPSO units are designed for the production and liquefaction of natural gas directly at sea, facilitating offshore gas field development. In contrast, FSRUs are utilized for receiving, storing, and regasifying LNG, enabling efficient supply to onshore pipelines. Both types enhance flexibility and accessibility in global gas markets, catering to diverse demand scenarios and reducing infrastructure investments.</p></p>
