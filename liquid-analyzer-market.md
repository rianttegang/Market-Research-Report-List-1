<p><p>The Liquid Analyzer market encompasses various types used for water quality monitoring and industrial processes. Turbidity analyzers measure water clarity, while pH/ORP analyzers assess acidity and oxidation levels. TOC and COD analyzers evaluate organic carbon content and chemical oxygen demand, essential for wastewater treatment. Titro analyzers determine concentrations of specific substances through titration. Conductivity analyzers gauge water's ionic content, chlorine analyzers monitor disinfectant levels, and dissolved oxygen analyzers ensure adequate oxygen for aquatic life, each playing a crucial role in environmental and operational safety.</p></p>
