<p><strong>The VOC Measurement Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC measurement device market encompasses various technologies, notably PID (Photoionization Detector) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID devices utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules, offering high sensitivity to a wide range of volatile organic compounds, making them suitable for continuous monitoring. Conversely, MOS sensors operate by detecting changes in electrical resistance when gases interact with a heated metal oxide layer, providing a less costly solution, ideal for outdoor or general air quality applications. Both technologies play crucial roles in environmental monitoring and industrial safety.</p></p>
