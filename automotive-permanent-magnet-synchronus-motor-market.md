<p><strong>The Automotive Permanent Magnet Synchronus Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface PMSM (SPMSM) Type</li><li>Interior PMSM (IPMSM) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive permanent magnet synchronous motor (PMSM) market is primarily divided into two types: Surface PMSM (SPMSM) and Interior PMSM (IPMSM). SPMSMs feature magnets located on the rotor's surface, offering high efficiency and straightforward manufacturing, making them suitable for applications demanding compact designs. In contrast, IPMSMs integrate magnets within the rotor, allowing for better torque density and performance at higher speeds, making them ideal for more demanding automotive applications. Both types contribute significantly to the growing electric vehicle market.</p></p>
