<p><p>The Virtual Commissioning market encompasses two primary types: Plant and Process Simulation, and Robotics and Automation Simulation. </p><p>Plant and Process Simulation focuses on modeling entire production systems to optimize operations, reduce downtime, and validate processes before physical implementation. In contrast, Robotics and Automation Simulation emphasizes the testing and programming of robotic systems and automated machinery, ensuring seamless integration and performance. Both types enhance efficiency, reduce risks, and enable companies to visualize and validate their systems in a virtual environment prior to real-world deployment.</p></p>
