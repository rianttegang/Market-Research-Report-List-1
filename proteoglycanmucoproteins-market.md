<p><p>Proteoglycans, often categorized as mucoproteins, are complex macromolecules that play crucial roles in various biological processes, including cell signaling, tissue hydration, and structural integrity of the extracellular matrix. Composed of a core protein covalently bonded to glycosaminoglycan (GAG) chains, they are essential in maintaining healthy cartilage and connective tissues. These versatile molecules are increasingly recognized for their therapeutic potential in areas such as regenerative medicine, osteoarthritis treatment, and dermatology.</p><p>The Proteoglycan (Mucoproteins) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period, driven by rising demand for advanced biomaterials in medical applications.
