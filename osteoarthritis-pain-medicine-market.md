<p><p>The osteoarthritis pain medicine market is segmented into three main types: oral, injection, and external treatments. Injection therapies often involve corticosteroids or hyaluronic acid injections directly into the joint to provide targeted relief. External treatments encompass topical analgesics and creams applied to the skin, offering localized pain relief with minimal systemic effects. Each type caters to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
