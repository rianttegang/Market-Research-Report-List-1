<p><p>Wire thread inserts are essential components used to enhance the strength and durability of threaded holes in various materials. The two main types are free-running inserts and screw-locking inserts. Free-running inserts allow for smooth movement of screws and bolts, ideal for general applications. In contrast, screw-locking inserts provide added resistance to unscrewing due to vibrations, making them suitable for high-stress environments. Both types cater to different needs in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, ensuring secure and reliable connections.</p></p>
