<p><strong>The Dental Implants & Prosthetics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endosteal Implants</li><li>Subperiosteal Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dental Implants and Prosthetics market focuses on two primary types: endosteal and subperiosteal implants. Endosteal implants are surgically placed directly into the jawbone, providing a stable foundation for prosthetic teeth, ideal for patients with sufficient bone structure. In contrast, subperiosteal implants are positioned under the gum but above the jawbone, suitable for patients with inadequate bone density who cannot undergo bone grafting. Both types aim to restore functionality and aesthetics in dental rehabilitation.</p></p>
