<p><strong>The Human Immunodeficiency Virus Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Antibody Tests</li><li>RNA (viral load) Test</li><li>Antibody-antigen Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Human Immunodeficiency Virus (HIV) testing market encompasses three main types: antibody tests, RNA (viral load) tests, and antibody-antigen tests. Antibody tests detect antibodies produced in response to HIV, indicating past or ongoing infection. RNA tests measure the actual viral load in the blood, providing early detection and monitoring of HIV progression. Antibody-antigen tests combine both antibody detection and p24 antigen assessment, allowing for earlier diagnosis after exposure. Each test type plays a crucial role in HIV management and prevention.</p></p>
